The Science Behind Weight Loss

This blog tells us about Weight loss is governed by the balance between calories consumed and burned, with a caloric deficit leading to weight loss and a surplus causing weight gain. Metabolism, the process of converting food into energy, plays a key role and is influenced by factors like age, muscle mass, and activity levels. The body’s energy needs include basal metabolic rate (BMR), physical activity, and digestion. Sustainable weight loss requires managing calorie intake through nutrient-rich foods, increasing calorie burn through exercise, and addressing factors like hormones, sleep, and stress, which impact hunger, fat storage, and metabolic efficiency.
